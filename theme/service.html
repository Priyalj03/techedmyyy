Internships

We partner with leading IT companies to offer students internships that enhance their practical knowledge and technical skills. During the internship, students:

  • Apply classroom concepts to solve actual business problems.
  • Gain exposure to the latest industry tools and methodologies.
  • Work under mentorship of IT professionals.
  • Receive career growth guidance.
  • Build a project portfolio to strengthen their resume.

Job Placements

Our IT training program doesn't just end with skill development; we take it a step further by offering comprehensive placement support. Through our network of industry contacts and partner companies, we provide:

  • Job matching
  • Resume and LinkedIn optimization
  • Mock interviews
  • Direct placement assistance

Aptitude Training

Aptitude tests are a critical component of the recruitment process, especially in the IT sector. To ensure that our students excel in these tests, we offer specialized aptitude training that focuses on:

  • Quantitative reasoning
  • Logical reasoning
  • Verbal ability
  • Coding aptitude
  • Practice sessions and mock tests

Soft Skills Training

Our soft skills training equips students with the ability to communicate effectively, collaborate with teams, and present themselves professionally. Key areas of focus include:

  • Communication skills
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Presentation skills
  • Time management and organization
